The Vandrevala Foundation is committed to making mental health services accessible to all. They offer helplines, counseling, and awareness programs.

iCall

iCall is a telephonic and email counseling service provided by the Tata Institute of Social Sciences (TISS). They offer confidential and free counseling.

Roshni

Roshni is a Hyderabad-based NGO that focuses on suicide prevention. They provide a helpline for individuals in emotional distress.

Richmond Fellowship Society

The Richmond Fellowship Society works towards the rehabilitation of individuals with mental illness. They provide various support services.

Sangath

Sangath is a non-profit organization that focuses on mental health and developmental challenges. They conduct research and provide community-based services.

Contact: sangath@sangath.in

Snehi, based in Delhi, works towards the well-being of children and adolescents. They provide counseling and support services for mental health issues.
